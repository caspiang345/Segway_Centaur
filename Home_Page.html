Meet the New Face of Motion

Motion. The mythical centaur was a creature that embodied the intellect of man and the speed of a horse in the time of the ancient Greeks. Skip forward two milenia and the new face of speed is a car. Problem is, cars are big, expensive, and environmentally damaging. To fix these problems, we came up with the Segway Centaur. This machine was made to be a lighter, cheaper, and environmentally friendly alternative for those who want to get around.
